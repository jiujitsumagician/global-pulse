## Long description
Turn your TV into a living window on the world.

Global Pulse is a beautiful 3D-globe screensaver that tracks real events around
the planet in real time. A photoreal Earth rotates through space, then stops and
zeroes in on each unfolding story — showing a headline, a photo, the location,
and the time — before gliding on to the next.

It pulls from live global sources around the clock:
• Breaking world news from major outlets
• Earthquakes (USGS)
• Natural events and disasters (NASA EONET)
• Severe weather alerts
• Rocket launches and spaceflight

Every marker is a real event, plotted exactly where it's happening. The globe
flies to each one, holds while you take it in, then moves on — a calm, endlessly
changing view of the world that's never the same twice.

Sit back and watch the world turn.
