## Long description
Turn your Fire TV into a living window on the world.

Global Pulse is a beautiful 3D-globe app that tracks real events around the
planet in real time. A photoreal Earth rotates through space, then flies to each
unfolding story — showing a headline, a photo, the location, and the time —
before gliding on to the next.

It pulls from live global sources around the clock:
• Breaking world news from major outlets
• Earthquakes (USGS)
• Natural events and disasters (NASA EONET)
• Severe weather alerts
• Rocket launches and spaceflight

Every marker is a real event, plotted exactly where it's happening. The globe
flies to each one, holds while you take it in, then moves on. Press OK on any
story to open the full article right on your TV.

Leave it running as an ambient world view, or lean in and explore what's
happening on Earth right now. It's never the same twice.

Sit back and watch the world turn.
