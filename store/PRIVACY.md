# Global Pulse — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-30_

Global Pulse is a screensaver that displays a live feed of public world events on
your TV. We designed it to collect as little as possible.

## What we collect
**Nothing personal.** Global Pulse does not require an account, does not ask you
to sign in, and does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable
information. There is no advertising and no third-party tracking in the app.

## How it works
To show live events, the app requests a public, read-only feed of world events
(news headlines, earthquakes, natural events, severe weather, and rocket
launches) from our service at `global-pulse-two.vercel.app`. Event headline
images are loaded from their original news sources. When you open an event as a
QR code (channel mode only), the QR image is generated by a public QR service.

These requests are standard internet requests required to display content. We do
not associate them with you, and we do not build a profile of you.

## Device and platform data
Roku may collect device and usage information under Roku's own privacy policy as
part of operating your device and the Roku platform. That collection is governed
by Roku, not by Global Pulse. See Roku's privacy policy for details.

## Children
Global Pulse is a general-audience screensaver and does not knowingly collect any
information from anyone, including children.
